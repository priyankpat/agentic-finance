## 🎯 7 SPECIALIZED AGENTS

### **1. Data Ingestion Agent** 🔄
- **Role**: Chief Data Officer
- **Responsibility**: Parse emails, receipts, APIs
- **Model**: Haiku (cost-efficient)
- **Cost**: $0.0008/call
- **Features**: Email parsing, OCR, API integration, validation

### **2. Expense Categorizer** 📊
- **Role**: Financial Analyst
- **Responsibility**: Smart transaction categorization
- **Model**: Haiku + rule-based (70% free)
- **Cost**: $0.0002/call (optimized)
- **Features**: ML classification, anomaly detection, budget tracking

### **3. Portfolio Manager** 💼
- **Role**: Chief Investment Officer
- **Responsibility**: Multi-brokerage aggregation
- **Model**: Sonnet (balanced)
- **Cost**: $0.015/call
- **Features**: Holdings aggregation, performance tracking, rebalancing

### **4. Market Research** 🔬
- **Role**: Senior Analyst
- **Responsibility**: Market data + fundamental analysis
- **Model**: Sonnet (with batching)
- **Cost**: $0.005/stock (batched)
- **Features**: Technical analysis, sentiment, macro indicators

### **5. Investment Recommendation** 🎯
- **Role**: Investment Strategist
- **Responsibility**: AI-driven stock picking
- **Model**: Opus (highest quality)
- **Cost**: $0.030/call (2x/week only)
- **Features**: Buy/sell signals, risk scoring, backtesting

### **6. Risk Assessment** ⚠️
- **Role**: Risk Manager
- **Responsibility**: Portfolio risk analysis
- **Model**: Sonnet (with caching)
- **Cost**: $0.015/call
- **Features**: VaR, stress testing, concentration analysis

### **7. Reporting & Insights** 📈
- **Role**: Communications Officer
- **Responsibility**: Dashboards & reporting
- **Model**: Haiku (template generation)
- **Cost**: $0.0008/call
- **Features**: Reports, visualizations, alerts
